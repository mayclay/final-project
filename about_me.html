about me

I started working with ceramics in early 2024 and quickly fell in love with it. Since then I've spent my free time at the studio to learn as much as possible. I hope to improve my throwing and one day share my work with others. In my free time, I enjoy traveling, baking, cooking, trying new coffee beans, and hiking.
